About Me

Enjoy the time with your little ones

I am a certified baby and toddler sleep consultant based in Norwich with a rich history of satisfied parents. Please reach out to me for more information. I have been a nanny for over 20 years and have worked for many families in London and Norwich. I have 3 children and live in Norwich but previously in London. Since working with families that have had babies that don’t sleep very well it has given me the inspiration to study sleep in babies and toddlers. I am a Certified Sleep Consultant who is trained by Emma Purdue who is based in New Zealand.. It has been my privilege to have helped a number of parents who were struggling to get a good night sleep because their baby or toddler is constantly waking during the night. It is so important for babies and toddlers to get a good night sleep as this is a vital time for them to grow and it also will make them happier during the daytime. You really do not need to struggle and in return will make parenthood a much more enjoyable time for not just you but for your child as well.
